## Abstract
This project presents a comprehensive HR Analytics Dashboard developed using the **IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance Dataset** to uncover the key drivers of employee attrition, performance, and compensation. The dashboard leverages Power BI to transform raw HR data into actionable insights that support strategic workforce planning and evidence-based decision-making.

Through interactive visualizations, the analysis explores how demographic factors, job characteristics, overtime, commute distance, marital status, education level, and tenure influence employee turnover and performance outcomes. The findings highlight high-risk employee segments, compensation disparities, and performance trends, enabling organizations to design targeted retention strategies, improve employee satisfaction, and strengthen overall workforce stability.

## Key Insights

### Attrition Insights
- Overall attrition rate is **16%**.  
- Sales and R&D show the highest turnover.

### Employee Segmentation Insights
- Single employees show the highest attrition.  
- Married employees have the lowest turnover.

### Overtime & Satisfaction
- Overtime strongly correlates with higher attrition and lower satisfaction.

### Education & Income
- Higher education levels lead to higher income.

### Performance, Tenure & Compensation
- High performers cluster at higher income and longer tenure.

### Age Group Attrition
- Employees aged 18â€“25 have the highest attrition.

### Distance From Home
- Employees living 21+ miles away show the highest attrition.

## Recommendations

### Improve retention in high-risk departments
- Conduct exit interviews  
- Review workload and leadership issues  
- Implement targeted retention programs  

### Address overtime-related burnout
- Introduce workload balancing  
- Encourage flexible schedules  
- Monitor overtime hours  

### Support younger employees
- Provide career development programs  
- Offer mentorship  
- Improve onboarding  

### Reduce long-distance commute stress
- Offer remote/hybrid work options  
- Provide transportation support  

### Reward and retain high performers
- Implement performance-based incentives  
- Create clear promotion pathways
